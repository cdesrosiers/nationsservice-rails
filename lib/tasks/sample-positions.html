Fellowships

New to Nations' Service? Check out our How-To Guide:

+ How-To Guide for Adding and Editing Fellowships and Internships
- How-To Guide for Adding and Editing Fellowships and Internships

Step 1.

Create a Wikidot account and become a member of Nations’ Service.

All members can add and edit fellowship and internship opportunities.

Step 2.

Find an opportunity to add and enter the full name of the fellowship/internship into the bar above (next to the Add button) and click the button.

These lists are for opportunities that include at least a living stipend – please do not include any unpaid fellowships or internships.

Step 3.

Complete the information in the fellowship/internship form that will display on your new page.

- Please include deadline information and mark N/A if rolling or not applicable and upload a logo to display on the page.

- The description can be copied from the fellowship/internship information website. The description box allows wiki-formatting, for use if you feel comfortable with it (a guide can be found under Info>Wiki Syntax)

- Please indicate whether the opportunity is only available to students at a specific school or if it is not school specific.

- Also indicate via the checkboxes whether the opportunity is available to students who have graduated (it will show up on the fellowships page), undergraduates (it will show up on the internships page), or both (it will show up on both pages).

Step 4.

Save the page. Then add tags by clicking the Tags button at the bottom of your created page. Tags are used for keywords and cities.

Editing

Wikis work best when a community of contributors is constantly correcting and revising content.

If you see mistakes or out-of-date information please update the page – it’s quick and easy.
